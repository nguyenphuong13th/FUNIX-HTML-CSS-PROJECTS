Where is Buu Long?

Buu Long Scenic Landscape Area is located to the northwest of Bien Hoa city, on the left bank of the peaceful and poetic Dong Nai river, in Buu Long ward - where there is a famous traditional stone carving profession in the country. From the center of Bien Hoa city, visitors follow Huynh Van Nghe street (Bien Hoa direction - Vinh Cuu) about 4km.

  • Address: KP 4 , Huynh Van Nghe street,Buu Long ward,Bien Hoa city
