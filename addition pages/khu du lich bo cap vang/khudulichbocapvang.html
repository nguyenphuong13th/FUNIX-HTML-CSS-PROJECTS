Where is Bo Cap Vang?

Bo Cap Vang tourist area is located in Hamlet 3, Phuoc Khanh Commune, Nhon Trach District, Dong Nai Province. From Ho Chi Minh City, young people only take 1 hour by motorbike to go to Bo Cap Vang, so this is the place chosen by the majority of young people when they want to find a large natural place with games as well as attractive exercises.

  • Address: Hamlet 3, Phuoc Khanh Commune, Nhon Trach District, Dong Nai Province,Viet Nam.
