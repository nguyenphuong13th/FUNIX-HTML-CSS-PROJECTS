Where is Suoi Mo?

Suoi Mo Park is an eco-tourism area you cannot ignore when traveling to Dong Nai. Only about 100km from the center of Saigon, this place is a place to attract young people to organize fun outdoor learning sessions, and also a place for families to choose to rest and enjoy the green space. to each last week.
